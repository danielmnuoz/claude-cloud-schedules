Iran War & Middle East (Day 40 — Developing)

  • A two-week US-Iran ceasefire brokered by Pakistan took effect April 8, hours before Trump's deadline for Iran to reopen the Strait of Hormuz or face further strikes; oil tankers briefly resumed transit through the strait.
  • The truce is already under strain: Iran halted Hormuz oil traffic again within hours, citing Israeli airstrikes on Lebanon — which the US and Israel say are not covered by the ceasefire agreement, in direct contradiction to mediator Pakistan's position.
